About Thai Cuisine

Thai cuisine is known for its balance of five fundamental flavors in each dish or the overall meal - hot (spicy), sour, sweet, salty, and bitter (optional).

Our chefs use authentic ingredients imported directly from Thailand to ensure the most genuine taste experience.

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Tom Yum Goong (ต้มยำกุ้ง) - Spicy Shrimp Soup
  • Pad Thai (ผัดไทย) - Stir-Fried Noodles
  • Som Tum (ส้มตำ) - Spicy Green Papaya Salad
  • Gaeng Daeng (แกงแดง) - Red Curry
  • Khao Niew Mamuang (ข้าวเหนียวมะม่วง) - Mango Sticky Rice
